### Bridging

Bridging is essential for transferring tokens between different blockchains.
This allows you to utilize tokens from various chains on the SHE network in
their wrapped forms. It's crucial to verify the compatibility of the wrapped
versions of your tokens on the SHE chain.

For the best bridging experience, visit the
[SHE Bridge page](https://app.she.io/bridge).

## Testnet and Devnet
