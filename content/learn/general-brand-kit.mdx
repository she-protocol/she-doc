SHE’s brand is inspired by Japanese Zen gardens, reflecting harmony, simplicity,
and mindfulness. The angular and minimalistic design conveys speed, efficiency,
and tranquility, aligning with SHE’s commitment to high performance and ease of
use in the blockchain space.
